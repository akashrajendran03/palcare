We're Comfycare Products Private Limited
An initiative in its nascent juncture

The stimulus to forming this company stems from a conviction to serve individuals seeking medical aid to transition into a smoother and more comfortable lifestyle. With this vision in mind, our founding members ventured into serving whilst ensuring and securing the highest possible standards of quality and compliance to health regulatory norms.Our experience of over 36 years in the pharmaceutical industry has helped us gain a better perception. We continue to be involved with three reputed International companies in the generic, cardio metabolic, mother and child and cancer treatment segments.

We've Our Brand Palcare
We Seek to leverage our capability

Through our brand Palcare ; we seek to leverage our capability to bridge the gap between customer needs and expectations, developments in the field of medicine and healthcare services, price – quality trade off, and technical functional strains of the industry.Our products are still in its nascent stages of differentiation – but with your continued faith in us; we are sure to expand and cater to a wider gamut of product ranges.
